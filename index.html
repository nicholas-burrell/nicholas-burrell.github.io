About Me

I diagnose and resolve hardware and software issues across various platforms, ensuring optimal system functionality and customer satisfaction. I also provide technical support and training to end-users, as well as preventive maintenance and security updates to systems.

Beyond the realm of technology, I proudly volunteered for my Church, embarking on a two-year journey in the Philippines. This period not only broadened my cultural horizons but also instilled in me a sense of compassion and adaptability. I also became fluent in Cebuano, creating connections through language and understanding.

I love the outdoors, whether it's hiking along scenic trails with my wife and daughter or carving through the snow on a snowboard.
